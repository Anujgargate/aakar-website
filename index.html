About AAKAR

AAKAR Engineering Services is an India-based industrial automation company with 15+ years of experience. We design and build CE-ready assembly and test stations for OEM production environments.

Our engineering focus is on safety, repeatability, and disciplined execution, enabling predictable delivery and long-term partnerships.

Our Capabilities

Assembly Stations

Manual, semi-automatic and automatic stations with fixture-based part mounting and validation.

Test Stations

Functional testing, leak testing, end-of-line inspection and result validation.

Small Automation Lines

Modular lines built using standardized stations for scalable production.

Engineering Approach

  • Standardized safety and machine architecture
  • Fixed electrical and PLC design philosophy
  • Reusable software and alarm structures
  • Process-specific fixtures, tooling and sequences

Safety & CE Philosophy

  • Risk assessment – EN ISO 12100
  • Electrical safety – EN 60204-1
  • Functional safety approach – ISO 13849
  • Integrated guarding, interlocks and emergency stops

Delivery & FAT Process

  1. Scope freeze & design approval
  2. Engineering and assembly
  3. Factory Acceptance Test (FAT)
  4. Dispatch after FAT approval
